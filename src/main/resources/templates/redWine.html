Meier's Sweet Red Wine

This is a semi-sweet wine made from the Concord grape. Enjoy the fresh fruit and slight effervescence. Although an excellent wine with most foods, this wine is also perfect to sip while relaxing after any meal. Serve chilled.

Radius Cabernet

This delicious Cabernet greets you with aromas of red jammy fruits and black cherry. Hints of toasty oak lead to a delightful finish. A true crowd-pleaser, this is great for entertaining and pairs well with hors d'oeuvres

Mascota Vineyards Unanime

A blend of cabernet sauvignon, cabernet franc and malbec, this delivers beautiful fruit with berry and lemon rind character and hints of toasted oak. Lovely flavorful finish. Delicious.

Warm-N-Cozy Mulled Wine

The welcoming scent of cinnamon and spice will make you "warm and cozy" on even the coldest of winter days. To serve, empty contents into pot and warm to your preference. Garnish with cinnamon sticks or a slice of orange.

#520 Dry Syrah Red Wine

This dry Syrah is a medium to full-bodied red wine with medium-plus to high levels of tannins and flavors of blackberry, mint and black pepper notes.

#4 Pinot Noir Spätburgunder Classic, Dry

A wine with buttery characteristics has been aged in oak and generally is rich and flat (less Acidity). A buttery wine often has a cream-like texture that hits the middle of your tongue almost like oil (or butter) and has a smooth finish.
